About Us

Kingsmen Capital Corporation LLC is a real estate solutions company working with homeowners, goals. Through our extensive knowledge of the business, network of resources, and investment expertise, we are able to assist homeowners with a wide variety of real estate problems. We work one-on-one with each customer to handle their unique individual situations. With the ability to directly purchase homes and make cash offers, we can create an extremely fast, and hassle-free transaction. In addition, we re-develop a large number of single family and multi-family properties throughout the Southern California with the intention of encouraging home ownership while making it quick and simple.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide real estate solutions while helping to revitalize neighborhoods and increase the standard of living by improving the overall quality of housing for the residents. Call us today and let’s see how we can work together.
